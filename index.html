About us

Built for honest, fast returns

KindReturn QR helps a finder reach the owner quickly by combining contact details, map guidance, and a mobile-friendly scan flow in one lightweight web app.

The app is designed for simple deployment on Vercel with optional Supabase storage for scan history.
